Fleabag & The Priest

It probably isn't any surprise that Fleabag herself is the most hysterical character. From breaking the fourth wall to her sarcastic side comments to saying just about anything on her mind, Fleabag is as awkward and cringy as she is totally funny. Fans will never forget her confession at the circle or her worst moments with her Godmother or the Bank Manager. Fleabag is just funny in her nature, and while she might actually be the only character that tries to be, it certainly works. It isn't just Fleabag who has fallen head over heels for the Priest. He's not only handsome, but he's intelligent, fun, kind, and absolutely hilarious. He's quick-witted, and totally confident in throwing out some sarcasm and quips. From his obsession with the Priest's clothing to his banter with Fleabag, the Priest is beyond easy to laugh with - and at. It's no doubt he's one of the funniest characters the series has to offer.

Storyline

A dry-witted woman, known only as Fleabag, has no filter as she navigates life and love in London while trying to cope with tragedy.The angry, grief-riddled woman tries to heal while rejecting anyone who tries to help her, but Fleabag continues to keep up her bravado through it all.

Comic actress Phoebe Waller-Bridge stars as the titular character on the series, which is based on Waller-Bridge's 2013 one-woman show of the same name.
